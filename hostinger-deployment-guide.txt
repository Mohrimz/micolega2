# Hostinger Free Hosting Deployment Guide

## Step 1: Sign Up
- Go to: https://www.hostinger.com
- Click "Free Hosting" or go to https://www.000webhost.com
- Sign up (no card required for free plan)

## Step 2: Create Website  
- Choose "Upload your own website"
- Website name: micolega (or your choice)
- Domain will be: micolega.000webhostapp.com

## Step 3: File Manager Access
- Go to your Hostinger dashboard
- Click "File Manager" 
- You'll see public_html folder

## Step 4: Upload Structure
Upload files in this structure:
```
public_html/
├── index.php (use index-hostinger.php)
├── .htaccess (copy from public/.htaccess)
├── css/ (copy from public/css)
├── js/ (copy from public/js)
├── images/ (copy from public/images)
└── laravel/
    ├── app/
    ├── bootstrap/
    ├── config/
    ├── database/
    ├── resources/
    ├── routes/
    ├── storage/
    ├── vendor/
    ├── .env
    ├── composer.json
    └── artisan
```
